Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 ripe banana (mashed)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter, then mix well.
  3. In another bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  4. Combine both mixtures.
  5. Stir until smooth.
  6. Heat a pan on medium heat.
  7. Pour mixture into the pan.
  8. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  9. Cook until golden brown and serve.
