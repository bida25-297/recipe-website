Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • ½ cup fresh blueberries

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  5. Heat a pan on medium heat.
  6. Pour batter into the pan.
  7. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  8. Cook the other side until golden brown.
  9. Serve warm with syrup or extra berries.
