Chocolate pancake

ingredients

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour,2 tablespoons cocoa powder,2 tablespoons sugar,1 teaspoon baking powder, 1 cup milk,1 egg, 2 tablespoons melted butter or oil,1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. 1.In a bowl,mix flour,cocoa powder,sugar and baking powder. 2.In another bowl,whisk milk,egg,butter and vanilla. 3.Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients. 4.Mix well until smooth (no lumps). 5.Heat a pan on medium heat. 6.Pour some butter into the pan. 7.Cook until bubbles form then flip. 8.Cook the other side until done. 9.Serve warm with chcolate syrup or toppings of your choice and enjoy.
