Chocolate pancake

ingredients

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg>
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter or oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. 1.In a bowl mix flour,cocoa powder,sugar and baking powder.
  2. 2.In another bowl,whisk milk,egg,butter and vanilla.
  3. 3.Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients.
  4. 4.Mix well until smooth (no lumps).
  5. 5.Heat a pan on medium heat.
  6. 6.Apply some butter into the pan.
  7. 7. Pour the mixtue in the pan and cook until bubbles form then flip.
  8. 8.Cook the other side until done.
  9. 9.Serve warm with chcolate syrup or toppings of your choice and enjoy.
